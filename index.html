Additional Information

BBG has an enormous collection of living plants spread throughout the gardens. Most collections contain a wide variety of genera and this graphic enables you to view the variety of specimens in each collection.

How to use this visualization:

  • The tree expands from a garden collection, to genera, and finally to an individual plant specimen.
  • Click on a circle node to explore the next layer of data.
